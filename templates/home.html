Welcome to the Employee Management System

Our Employee Management System is designed to streamline the process of managing employee information, departments, and posts within your organization. With our system, you can easily add, edit, and delete employee records, assign employees to departments and posts, and manage the hierarchy of managers and department heads.

Features

  • Manage employee information including personal details, contact information, and employment details.
  • Organize employees into departments and posts.
  • Assign managers and department heads to oversee teams and departments.
  • View and manage the hierarchy of employees and managers.

Get Started

To get started, use the navigation menu to explore the different sections of the system. You can view and manage departments, posts, and managers, or add new employees to the system.
